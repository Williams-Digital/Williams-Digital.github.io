Our Mission

Brewing Change, One Cup at a Time

Empower Café operates as more than just a café. We're a workforce training program where individuals with disabilities and employment barriers gain real-world skills through food service and retail training.

Our trainees earn ServSafe certifications, build teamwork and communication skills, and develop a path toward independence — all while serving incredible food to our community.
