Silver Sneakers

Stay active, build strength, and connect with your community. Fitness has no age limit at Vestavia Hills Parks & Recreation.

Program Overview

Your Path to Active Living

A welcoming fitness program designed with older adults in mind, open to participants of all ages and ability levels.

Silver Sneakers at Vestavia Hills is a cardio and strength class that combines low-impact aerobic exercise with muscle-strengthening activities. Each session is led by a certified instructor who tailors movements to suit every fitness level.

Whether you are just beginning your fitness journey or looking to maintain an active lifestyle, Silver Sneakers offers a supportive, encouraging environment where you can move at your own pace and feel great doing it.

SilverSneakers membership is accepted and is available free through many Medicare Advantage and Medicare Supplement plans. Not sure if you are covered? Bring your insurance card and we will help you find out.

No registration is required. Simply show up, sign in, and join the class. New participants are always welcome.

What to Expect

Class Details

Each session blends four key elements for a well-rounded, enjoyable workout experience.

Low-Impact Cardio

Gentle aerobic movements that elevate your heart rate safely, improving endurance and circulation without stressing your joints.

Strength Training

Build and maintain muscle using resistance bands and light weights. All exercises offer modifications to match your current fitness level.

Balance & Flexibility

Dedicated stretching and balance exercises help prevent falls, improve posture, and increase your range of motion for everyday activities.

Social Community

More than just exercise, Silver Sneakers is a welcoming community. Build friendships, share laughs, and enjoy the energy of working out together.

Why Participate

Benefits of Silver Sneakers

Regular participation delivers lasting improvements to your physical health, mental well-being, and social life.

1

Cardiovascular Health

Low-impact aerobic exercise strengthens your heart, lowers blood pressure, and improves circulation for long-term wellness.

2

Strength & Mobility

Resistance training preserves muscle mass, supports bone density, and keeps you moving confidently through daily activities.

3

Social Connections

Join a supportive group of neighbors and friends. The social aspect of regular classes reduces isolation and boosts mental health.

4

Free with Insurance

SilverSneakers membership is included at no cost with many Medicare Advantage and Supplement plans, making fitness accessible for everyone.
