Youth Athletics

Flag Football

A fun, non-contact introduction to the game of football that teaches teamwork, sportsmanship, and the fundamentals of the sport.

Program Overview

Learn the Game the Safe Way

Our flag football program gives kids the thrill of football without the contact, building confidence and athletic skills in a supportive environment.

Ages 5 - 12, Co-Ed

Open to boys and girls, divided into age-appropriate divisions for fair and fun competition.

Fall Season: August - October

Eight weeks of practices and games held during the best weather of the year.

Wald Park Fields

Games and practices held at Vestavia Hills' premier outdoor athletic facility.

No Contact - Safe for Everyone

Flag-pulling replaces tackling, making this the ideal entry point into football.

Building Skills On and Off the Field

Our flag football program is designed to teach children the fundamentals of football in a safe, non-contact environment. Players learn passing, catching, route-running, and defensive positioning through structured practices and organized games each week.

Beyond athletic development, the program emphasizes sportsmanship, teamwork, and fair play. Coaches focus on creating a positive experience where every child gets meaningful playing time, regardless of skill level. It is about building character as much as it is about building athletes.

No experience necessary. Our volunteer coaches work with every skill level and ensure each player feels like an important part of the team.

What's Included

Everything They Need to Play

Registration covers all the essentials so your child can focus on having fun and learning the game.

Flag Belts & Equipment

Official flag belts, cones, and game footballs are all provided for practices and games.

Team Jerseys

Each player receives a team jersey to wear during games and to keep at the end of the season.

Trained Volunteer Coaches

All coaches complete background checks and training focused on positive youth development.

End-of-Season Jamboree

The season wraps up with a tournament-style jamboree and awards celebration for all players.
