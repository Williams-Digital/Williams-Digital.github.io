Swim Team & Lessons

From first splashes to competitive strokes, our certified instructors help swimmers of every age and ability level build confidence in the water.

Program Overview

Two Tracks, One Great Aquatic Program

Whether your child is touching water for the first time or racing toward personal bests, Vestavia Hills has the right program to keep them moving forward.

Learn-to-Swim Lessons

Progressive, age-appropriate classes that build water safety skills and stroke technique in a supportive, fun environment. Every level ends with a skills assessment so you can track real progress.

  • Parent & Tot (6 months – 3 years) — Water familiarization with a caregiver in the pool
  • Beginner (ages 3–5) — Floating, kicking, and basic breath control
  • Intermediate (ages 6–9) — Freestyle, backstroke, and treading water
  • Advanced (ages 10+) — All four competitive strokes, turns, and endurance

Competitive Swim Team

Join the Vestavia Hills Stingrays for a summer of teamwork, competition, and personal achievement. Our coaches focus on technique, sportsmanship, and having a blast at every practice.

  • Summer League — June through July competitive season
  • Practice Schedule — 4 days per week (Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri)
  • Weekend Meets — Home and away dual meets every Saturday
  • Ages 5–18 — Grouped by age for fair, fun competition

Wald Park Aquatic Center

Two full-size pools — a 6-lane competition pool and a separate shallow-water teaching pool. Both are heated and maintained to USA Swimming standards.

Certified Lifeguard Instructors

Every instructor holds current American Red Cross Lifeguard, CPR/AED, and Water Safety Instructor (WSI) certifications. Your child is always in safe hands.

What’s Included

Everything Your Swimmer Needs

No hidden fees or surprise costs. Every registration includes these essentials to set your swimmer up for success.

Certified Instructors

Red Cross WSI-certified coaches with years of experience teaching all ages and skill levels.

Small Class Sizes

Maximum 4:1 student-to-instructor ratio ensures personal attention and faster skill development.

Caps & Progress Reports

Team swim cap included with registration. Detailed progress reports after each session track your child's growth.

End-of-Summer Meet

All swimmers are invited to our championship meet — a fun celebration of everything they have accomplished this season.
