Summer Camps

Give your kids an unforgettable summer of adventure, creativity, and new friendships at Vestavia Hills Parks & Recreation.

Program Overview

Everything You Need to Know

Our summer camps offer a safe, structured, and exciting environment where kids build confidence, explore new interests, and make lasting memories.

Flexible Scheduling

Full-day camps run from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Half-day options available from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM for younger campers or those who prefer a shorter day.

Ages 5 through 14

Campers are grouped by age to ensure activities are age-appropriate, engaging, and challenging. Each group has dedicated counselors and tailored programming.

8-Week Sessions

Camp runs from the first week of June through the last week of July. Register for individual weeks or the full 8-week session at a discounted rate.

Wald Park & Civic Center

Activities take place at Wald Park for outdoor adventures and the Vestavia Hills Civic Center for indoor workshops, arts, and rainy-day programming.

Certified Staff

All camp counselors are certified in first aid and CPR. Every staff member has completed thorough background checks and safety training.

Affordable Rates

Weekly and full-session pricing with discounts for Vestavia Hills residents, siblings, and early registration. Financial assistance available upon request.

Camp Types

Find the Perfect Camp

Choose from six unique camp programs designed to spark curiosity, build skills, and create unforgettable memories for every camper.

⚽

Sports Camp

Ages 5 - 14

A high-energy camp focused on teamwork, sportsmanship, and athletic development across a range of popular sports.

  • Basketball, soccer, flag football, and more
  • Skill clinics and friendly tournaments
  • Swimming sessions at Wald Park pool
  • Team-building games and relay races
🏔

Adventure Camp

Ages 7 - 14

For thrill-seekers and explorers ready to push their boundaries with exciting outdoor challenges and new experiences.

  • Hiking, orienteering, and geocaching
  • Rock wall climbing and obstacle courses
  • Archery and outdoor survival basics
  • Weekly field trips to area adventure parks
🎨

Arts & Creativity

Ages 5 - 12

A vibrant camp where young artists explore painting, sculpture, theater, music, and creative expression in all forms.

  • Painting, drawing, and mixed media
  • Theater games and end-of-week showcase
  • Music exploration and songwriting
  • Pottery and sculpture workshops
🔬

STEM Explorer

Ages 6 - 13

Hands-on experiments, coding projects, and engineering challenges that make science and technology exciting and accessible.

  • Robotics building and programming
  • Science experiments and lab activities
  • Intro to coding with Scratch and Python
  • Engineering design challenges
🌳

Nature & Outdoors

Ages 5 - 12

Reconnect with the natural world through hands-on exploration, gardening, wildlife observation, and eco-friendly projects.

  • Nature hikes and wildlife identification
  • Gardening and plant science
  • Creek exploration and water ecology
  • Campfire stories and outdoor cooking
🏆

Leaders in Training

Ages 12 - 14

A leadership development program for older campers who want to grow their skills while mentoring younger participants.

  • Leadership workshops and public speaking
  • Mentoring and assistant counselor duties
  • Community service projects
  • College-prep and goal-setting sessions
