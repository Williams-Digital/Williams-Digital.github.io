Program Overview

Play Ball in Vestavia Hills

Our youth baseball and softball program is one of the most popular in the Birmingham area, offering age-appropriate divisions for players of all skill levels.

A tradition of excellence on the diamond

The Vestavia Hills Parks & Recreation youth baseball and softball program has been nurturing young athletes for over 30 years. Our program emphasizes fundamentals, sportsmanship, and a genuine love for the game, all while providing a safe and encouraging environment for kids to grow.

All games and practices take place at the Wald Park Ball Fields, featuring three premier playing surfaces with LED lighting for evening games, covered dugouts, electronic scoreboards, and a full concession stand. Our fields are maintained to the highest standards so every player gets a first-class experience.

Age Divisions

A Division for Every Player

Five age-based divisions ensure that every child plays with peers at a similar developmental stage, keeping the game fun and competitive.

⚾
T-Ball
Ages 4 – 5
Introduction to the game with hitting off a tee. Focus on basic fielding, throwing, and base running in a fun, supportive setting.
🏃
Coach Pitch
Ages 6 – 7
Coaches pitch to their own players, building on fundamentals. Players develop hand-eye coordination and learn game situations.
⚽
Minors
Ages 8 – 9
Player-pitch baseball begins here. Athletes refine pitching, catching, and batting skills with standard league rules introduced.
🏋
Majors
Ages 10 – 12
Full competitive baseball with official rules. Players are challenged with steal plays, advanced fielding, and strategic game situations.
💪
Seniors
Ages 13 – 14
The highest division offers an advanced competitive experience preparing athletes for high school-level play.

What's Included

Everything Your Player Needs

Registration covers all the essentials so families can focus on cheering from the stands.

🤺

Professional Coaching

Trained and background-checked coaches lead every team, delivering age-appropriate instruction focused on skill development and sportsmanship. Coaches participate in annual clinics to stay current on best practices.

👕

Team Uniforms

Every player receives a full team uniform including a jersey, cap, and socks in their team colors. Uniforms are yours to keep as a memento of the season. Additional gear is available at the concession stand.

🏆

End-of-Season Tournament

Each season culminates in an exciting double-elimination tournament where every team competes for the division championship. Trophies are awarded to champions and individual awards recognize outstanding players.

🍕

Post-Game Snack Schedule

A rotating snack schedule ensures every player gets a post-game treat. Families take turns providing snacks and drinks, building community spirit and making game days even more memorable for the kids.
