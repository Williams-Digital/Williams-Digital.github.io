Our Facilities

Modern Spaces for Active Living

From our flagship Wald Park to the historic Civic Center, explore the facilities that make our community thrive.

Flagship

Wald Park

Our premier recreation complex opened in 2021, featuring pools, sports fields, a Miracle League field, amphitheater, playground, and walking trails.

🏊 2 Pools ⚾ 3 Ball Fields 🏛 Amphitheater 🚶 Walking Trail ♥ Miracle League
Fitness

Civic Center

Full gymnasium, 3-lane walking track, weight room, cardio equipment, and multipurpose class spaces.

🏀 2 Courts 🏋 Weight Room 🚶 Walking Track
