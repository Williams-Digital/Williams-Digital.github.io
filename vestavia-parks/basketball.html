🏀 Youth Sports Program

Basketball Leagues

Boys and girls leagues for all skill levels. From kindergartners learning the fundamentals to 8th graders competing in fast-paced tournament play, there is a spot on the court for every young athlete in Vestavia Hills.

500+ Players Each Season
8 Age Divisions
2 Seasons Per Year
60+ Teams Formed

Program Overview

Everything You Need to Know

Our basketball program is designed to develop skills, build confidence, and foster a love for the game in a fun and supportive environment.

👥

Divisions

Separate leagues for boys and girls ensure fair, competitive, and inclusive play across all age groups.

  • Boys Recreational League
  • Boys Competitive League
  • Girls Recreational League
  • Girls Competitive League
🏋

Age Groups

Four age brackets allow players to compete with peers at a similar developmental level.

K - 2nd Grade 3rd - 4th Grade 5th - 6th Grade 7th - 8th Grade
📅

Seasons

Two seasons throughout the year give players the opportunity to stay active and improve year-round.

  • Winter Season: November through February
  • Summer Season: June through July
🏛

Location

All games and practices are held at the Vestavia Hills Civic Center Gymnasium, featuring two full-size basketball courts.

  • 2 full-size regulation courts
  • Electronic scoreboards
  • Climate-controlled facility
  • Spectator seating available
🏆

Recreational Division

The recreational division focuses on fundamentals, teamwork, and having fun. Teams are balanced through a draft process to ensure fair and enjoyable competition.

  • Emphasis on skill development
  • Balanced team drafting
  • Equal playing time for all
🔥

Competitive Division

For experienced players looking for a higher level of competition. Pre-formed teams compete against other select squads in the area.

  • Pre-formed team rosters
  • Advanced game strategy
  • Tournament-style playoffs

What's Included

More Than Just Games

Every registration comes with everything your young athlete needs for a complete basketball experience.

🙋

Certified Referees

Every game is officiated by trained and certified referees who ensure fair play, teach the rules, and keep games running smoothly.

👕

Team Jerseys

Each player receives a reversible team jersey with their number. Jerseys are yours to keep at the end of the season as a memento.

🏀

Skills Clinics

Pre-season skills clinics are included with registration. Coaches work on dribbling, shooting, passing, and defensive fundamentals.

🏆

Playoff Tournament

Every season ends with an exciting single-elimination playoff tournament. All teams qualify, and champions receive awards and trophies.
