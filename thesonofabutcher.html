Our Story

Born Out of
Passion for Craft

Born out of pandemic necessity and a lifelong love of quality food, The Son of a Butcher is the retail arm of Evans Meats — a Birmingham wholesaler that supplies many of the best restaurants in Alabama and nearby states.

We work with smaller producers that allow more access into the inner workings of their operations, ensuring every cut, wheel, and fillet meets our exacting standards.
