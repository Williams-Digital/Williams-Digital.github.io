Our Story

Shaping Stories
Since 1988

Founded by Bernard Telsey, The Telsey Office has grown from a one-person operation into one of the most prolific casting organizations in entertainment. With offices in New York City and Los Angeles, we bring together a team of over 25 casting professionals.

Our work spans Broadway, Off-Broadway, national tours, regional theatre, film, television, and commercials. We are dedicated to the art and craft of casting — finding the right person for every role, and championing diverse, authentic storytelling.

Fellowship Program

Miranda Family
Fellowship

The Telsey Office is proud to participate in the Miranda Family Fellowship, a program designed to support emerging artists and arts administrators from underrepresented communities.

"We believe the future of casting — and of the industry — depends on opening doors for the next generation of diverse talent behind and in front of the camera."

Fellows join a network of over 100 emerging professionals, receiving ongoing professional development, peer networking, mentorship, and coaching with industry leaders.

Submission Policy

How to Submit

The Telsey Office accepts headshot and resume submissions by mail only. We also welcome postcards and invitations to performances.

Please direct all submissions to our New York office at 1501 Broadway, Suite 510, New York, NY 10036.

No phone calls or drop-offs, please. We review all submissions received by mail.
