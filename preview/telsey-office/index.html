The Casting Process

How we find the right person for every role.

01

Discovery

We immerse ourselves in the material — understanding the world, the characters, and the creative team's vision before a single audition.

02

Curation

Drawing from decades of relationships with talent, agents, and managers, we assemble a curated pool of artists for every role.

03

The Room

Our casting sessions are designed to bring out the best in every performer. We create a space where artists can do their most authentic work.

04

The Match

Collaborating closely with directors and producers, we guide the final decisions that bring characters to life on stage and screen.

Our Story

Shaping Stories
Since 1988

Founded by Bernard Telsey, The Telsey Office has grown from a one-person operation into one of the most prolific casting organizations in entertainment. With offices in New York City and Los Angeles, we bring together a team of 31 casting professionals.

Our work spans Broadway, Off-Broadway, national tours, regional theatre, film, television, and commercials. We are dedicated to the art and craft of casting — finding the right person for every role, and championing diverse, authentic storytelling.

"Casting is detective work. You're looking for the truth of a character in the truth of a person."

Bernard Telsey

Fellowship Program

Miranda Family
Fellowship

The Telsey Office participates in the Miranda Family Fellowship, supporting emerging artists and arts administrators from underrepresented communities.

"The future of casting — and the industry — depends on opening doors for the next generation of diverse talent behind and in front of the camera."

Fellows join a network of 100+ emerging professionals, receiving professional development, mentorship, and coaching with industry leaders.
