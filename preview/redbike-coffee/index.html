Our Story

A Passion for Perfect Coffee

At Red Bike Coffee Company, we believe that great coffee is more than a beverage - it's an experience. From the moment we source our beans from the world's finest growing regions to the careful roasting process in our Irondale facility, every step is guided by our commitment to excellence.

We work directly with farmers who share our values, ensuring sustainable practices and fair compensation while delivering the highest quality beans to your cup.
