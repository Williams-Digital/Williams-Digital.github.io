Our Story

From Bean Counter to Bean Roaster

A journey fueled by passion, service, and the pursuit of the perfect cup.

The Beginning

A Life Brewed with Purpose

After retiring from the Air Force and leaving behind a 20-plus year career in accounting, I finally had the freedom to chase what had quietly been calling me for years: the art of great coffee.

Traveling the world with the military opened my eyes to extraordinary coffee cultures — from the bold espresso bars of Italy to the fragrant roadside stalls of Southeast Asia. Every cup told a story, and I wanted to tell my own.

— Heather

"Life's too short for bad coffee. Every morning deserves something extraordinary."

Our Craft

Distinctive & Bold

At Barking Bee, we source only the most exceptional green coffee beans from farms that share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Each batch is carefully roasted in small quantities to draw out the unique character of every origin.

We believe coffee should be an experience — not just a caffeine delivery system. Our roasting process honors the bean's natural complexity, creating cups that are rich, layered, and unforgettable.

What Drives Us
