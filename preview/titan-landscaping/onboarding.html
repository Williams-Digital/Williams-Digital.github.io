Section 02

Project Overview

Below are the three website packages we offer. Your selected tier will be marked during our kickoff call. Each tier is built on a foundation of clean code, mobile responsiveness, and modern design principles.

Tier 1 — Starter

Single-page website with hero section, services overview, contact section, and simple footer. Clean and professional with full mobile responsiveness. Ideal for establishing a strong first impression online.

Most Popular

Tier 2 — Professional

Enhanced single-page site featuring an animated hero, full services section, portfolio gallery, about section, contact form, and multi-column footer. Includes scroll animations and interactive elements for a polished, engaging experience.

Tier 3 — Premium

Full cinematic website experience with video hero background, animated statistics, complete portfolio grid, process timeline, client testimonials, parallax effects, and premium interactions throughout. The complete package for brands that demand the best.

Section 03

What We Need From You

To build the best possible website, we need the following materials and information. Please check off each item as you gather it and return the completed list to your project contact at Williams Digital.

  • High-resolution logo files (if different from designed logos)
  • Company description / about text (or approval to use drafted copy)
  • Service descriptions and pricing (if applicable)
  • Portfolio project photos (minimum 5–10 high-quality images)
  • Client testimonials (minimum 3, with permission to publish)
  • Team / owner photos
  • Contact information (phone, email, physical address, service area)
  • Social media links (Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
  • Domain name preference (or existing domain details)
  • Hosting preferences (or we can set up hosting on your behalf)
  • Any existing brand guidelines or color preferences
  • Preferred call-to-action (e.g., "Call Now", "Get a Free Quote", etc.)
Section 04

Project Timeline

Our standard project timeline is outlined below. Timely delivery of content and feedback ensures we stay on schedule and hit your launch date.

Week 1

Onboarding & Content Collection

Kickoff call, gather brand assets, finalize scope and content requirements.

Week 2

Design Mockup & Revisions

Visual design concepts presented for review. Up to 2 rounds of revisions included.

Week 3

Development & Build

Approved design brought to life with clean, performant code and responsive layouts.

Week 4

Testing, Revisions & Launch

Cross-browser testing, final client review, and go-live deployment.

Note: Tier 1 projects may complete in as few as 2–3 weeks. Tier 3 Premium projects, due to the scope of cinematic features and custom interactions, may require 5–6 weeks. A detailed schedule will be confirmed during the kickoff call.
Section 05

Communication & Revisions

Clear communication is the foundation of a successful project. Here is how we will work together throughout the build.

Primary Contact

All project communication will be handled via email or scheduled calls. Your dedicated point of contact will respond within one business day.

Revision Policy

Two rounds of design revisions are included in your package. Additional revisions beyond this are available at an hourly rate, quoted in advance.

Progress Updates

You will receive weekly progress updates with screenshots and staging links so you can see the build take shape in real time.

Final Approval

Written sign-off is required before we push the site live. No launch happens without your explicit approval.
