Scope of Services

Williams Digital ("Provider") agrees to provide web design and development services to Titan Landscaping & Construction ("Client") as outlined in the selected service tier and accompanying project proposal. Project Tiers & Deliverables

The Provider offers three (3) service tiers, each with a defined set of deliverables. The Client's selected tier determines the scope of work to be performed under this Agreement:

Tier Included Deliverables
Tier 1
Starter 		Single-page responsive website with up to four (4) content sections; one (1) round of design revisions; full mobile optimization; basic contact form integration; deployment to Client's hosting environment.
Tier 2
Professional 		Single-page responsive website with up to eight (8) content sections; portfolio or project gallery; two (2) rounds of design revisions; scroll-based animations; contact form integration; basic SEO configuration; deployment to Client's hosting environment.
Tier 3
Premium 		Full multi-section responsive website with unlimited content sections; video integration; portfolio or project gallery; testimonials section; advanced scroll and interaction animations; three (3) rounds of design revisions; comprehensive SEO optimization; analytics setup (e.g., Google Analytics); thirty (30) days of post-launch support; deployment to Client's hosting environment.
Change Orders: Any work requested by the Client that falls outside the defined scope of the selected tier shall constitute a change order. Change orders must be documented in writing, agreed upon by both Parties, and may be subject to additional fees and extended timelines.

Revisions & Changes

The number of design revision rounds included with each project is determined by the selected service tier: Tier 1 (Starter) includes one (1) round; Tier 2 (Professional) includes two (2) rounds; and Tier 3 (Premium) includes three (3) rounds of revisions.
A "revision round" is defined as a single consolidated set of feedback and requested changes submitted by the Client at one time. The Client is encouraged to compile all revision notes before submitting each round to maximize the value of included revisions.
Additional revision rounds requested beyond those included in the selected tier shall be billed at a rate of seventy-five dollars ($75.00) per hour, with a minimum charge of one (1) hour per additional revision round. The Client will be notified before additional charges are incurred.
Major scope changes—including but not limited to new page sections, additional features, or functionality not contemplated in the original agreement—shall require a formal written change order. Change orders will include a description of the additional work, associated costs, and any impact on the project timeline. Work on change orders shall not commence until the change order is signed by both Parties.
The Provider reserves the right to respectfully decline change requests that, in the Provider's professional judgment, would compromise the design integrity, user experience, or technical performance of the website. In such cases, the Provider will offer alternative solutions.
