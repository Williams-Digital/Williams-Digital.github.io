Your Event at Helen

Where Every Detail Matters

Whether you’re celebrating a milestone, hosting a rehearsal dinner, or bringing colleagues together, Helen’s private dining experience is designed to make every gathering extraordinary.

Our events team works closely with you to craft a custom menu, select wines, and tailor every element of the evening to your vision. From intimate dinners of 8 to receptions of up to 40 guests, we’ll create an experience as unique as the occasion.

What We Offer

The Details

Custom Menus

Work directly with Chef Rob to design a multi-course menu tailored to your event, featuring seasonal ingredients and signature preparations from the wood-fired grill.

Curated Wine Pairings

Our sommelier will craft a wine program perfectly paired to your menu, with options ranging from approachable selections to rare and cellar-worthy bottles.

Intimate Private Room

Our private dining room seats up to 24 guests for a seated dinner, with warm lighting, custom table settings, and a dedicated service team.

Full Buyout Available

For larger celebrations of up to 100 guests, a full restaurant buyout creates an unforgettable setting with complete exclusivity and personalized service.
