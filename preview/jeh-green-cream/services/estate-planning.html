Overview

Architect Your Enduring Legacy

Generational wealth is not an accident of fortune but the result of deliberate, sophisticated planning. Without a comprehensive estate plan, even substantial wealth can be diminished by taxation, contested by beneficiaries, or mismanaged by those who inherit it. The consequences of inaction are measured not in dollars lost, but in legacies unrealized.

JE Holdings crafts estate plans that go far beyond simple wills and basic trusts. We construct multi-layered succession architectures that account for federal and state estate taxes, generation-skipping transfer taxes, and the unique dynamics of family wealth. Our strategies ensure that control remains where you intend it, wealth transfers efficiently, and your philanthropic vision endures.

Whether you are establishing your first comprehensive plan or restructuring an existing estate to adapt to evolving tax law, we bring three decades of experience to every engagement, treating each family's legacy with the gravity it deserves.

Key Benefits

Why Estate Planning Matters

01

Tax Optimization

Minimize Estate & Gift Tax Exposure

Through strategic use of irrevocable trusts, grantor retained annuity trusts, charitable lead trusts, and other advanced vehicles, we systematically reduce your estate's tax burden, preserving more wealth for your intended beneficiaries.

02

Legacy Preservation

Multi-Generational Structures

We build dynasty trust structures and family governance frameworks that protect wealth not just for your children, but for grandchildren and beyond. These structures include spendthrift protections, incentive provisions, and trustee succession plans.

03

Control & Flexibility

Maintain Control While Transferring Wealth

Our estate plans are designed to give you maximum control during your lifetime while ensuring seamless transitions. We employ structures that allow you to retain authority, adjust provisions, and respond to changing family circumstances.

Our Process

How We Build Your Estate Plan

I

Estate Analysis

We conduct an exhaustive review of your current estate, including all assets, existing trusts, insurance policies, business interests, and beneficiary designations to understand the full picture.

II

Trust Architecture

Based on your goals and analysis, we design a comprehensive trust architecture that optimizes tax efficiency, protects assets, and ensures your wealth is distributed according to your precise intentions.

III

Document Preparation

We prepare all necessary legal documents including trust agreements, wills, powers of attorney, healthcare directives, and any ancillary documents required for complete coverage.

IV

Ongoing Review

Estate plans must evolve with changing laws and life circumstances. We provide regular reviews and updates to ensure your plan remains optimized and aligned with your current wishes.
