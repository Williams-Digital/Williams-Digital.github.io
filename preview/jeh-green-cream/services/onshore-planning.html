Overview

Domestic Protection, Sophisticated Execution

Not every protection strategy requires crossing international borders. Several US states have enacted legislation that creates remarkably strong asset protection frameworks, rivaling many offshore jurisdictions in their effectiveness while eliminating the complexities of international reporting requirements.

Nevada offers some of the strongest charging order protections in the nation. Wyoming provides unparalleled privacy through its LLC statutes. Delaware remains the gold standard for corporate governance. South Dakota has emerged as a premier trust jurisdiction with perpetual trust provisions and no state income tax on trust earnings. Each state offers distinct advantages, and the optimal choice depends on your specific situation.

JE Holdings structures domestic asset protection trusts, series LLCs, and multi-state entity architectures that leverage the best of each jurisdiction. Our onshore strategies provide robust protection with the simplicity and familiarity of operating entirely within the US legal system, with well-established precedents that have been tested and upheld in court.

Why Onshore Planning Matters

Leverage Favorable State Laws

Each protection-friendly state offers unique advantages. We analyze which combination of state laws best serves your needs, from Nevada's charging order protections to South Dakota's perpetual trust provisions and zero state income tax on trust earnings.

Onshore structures eliminate the administrative burden of international reporting. There are no FBAR filings, no FATCA compliance, and no Form 3520 obligations, reducing both cost and the risk of inadvertent reporting failures.
