Our Story

Crafting Living Masterpieces Since 2009

Founded by master landscape architect Jonathan Mercer, Prestige Grounds was born from a singular vision: to elevate outdoor spaces into works of art that inspire, rejuvenate, and endure. What began as a boutique design studio has grown into the region's most sought-after landscape architecture firm.

Every project we undertake is a collaboration between our award-winning design team, master craftsmen, and horticultural scientists. We don't simply install landscapes — we create living ecosystems that evolve with the seasons and mature with grace.

What We Offer

Premium Services

Comprehensive landscape solutions tailored to estates, luxury residences, and premier commercial properties.

Custom Landscape Design

Bespoke 3D-rendered landscape plans by our award-winning architects, from concept through detailed construction drawings.

Luxury Hardscaping

Natural stone patios, hand-laid retaining walls, bespoke walkways, and architectural features using imported materials.

Water Features & Ponds

Custom waterfalls, koi ponds, reflecting pools, and fountain installations that bring tranquility to your grounds.

Outdoor Kitchens

Fully equipped outdoor culinary spaces with stone countertops, built-in grills, pizza ovens, and bar seating areas.

Landscape Lighting

Architectural LED systems that illuminate pathways, accent specimen trees, and create dramatic ambiance for evening enjoyment.

Irrigation Management

Smart Wi-Fi-connected irrigation systems with weather sensors, drip zones, and seasonal programming for optimal water efficiency.

Estate Grounds Maintenance

White-glove weekly maintenance programs for estates, including mowing, pruning, fertilization, and seasonal bed rotations.

Seasonal Color Programs

Curated annual and perennial rotation programs ensuring year-round color, texture, and visual interest across your property.

Sustainable Landscapes

Native plantings, rain gardens, bioswales, and xeriscaping designed to reduce environmental impact while maximizing beauty.

Snow & Ice Management

Commercial-grade snow removal, de-icing, and winter property protection with 24/7 on-call service during storm events.

How We Work

Our Refined Process

Every masterpiece begins with a conversation. Our four-phase approach ensures exceptional results every time.

1

Consultation

We visit your property, listen to your vision, assess the site conditions, and discuss budget parameters during an in-depth discovery session.

2

Design

Our architects create detailed 3D renderings, plant palettes, material boards, and phased construction documents for your approval.

3

Build

Our master craftsmen bring the design to life with meticulous attention to detail, premium materials, and constant project oversight.

4

Maintain

Our dedicated grounds team provides ongoing care with seasonal adjustments, ensuring your landscape matures beautifully year after year.
