Broadcast Safe Colors
Broadcast safe colors stay within the 16-235 RGB range (NTSC/PAL). Values below 16 or above 235 may clip on broadcast monitors. IRE levels: Black = 7.5 IRE (NTSC) / 0 IRE (PAL), White = 100 IRE.
