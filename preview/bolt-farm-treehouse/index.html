Our Story

Built by Hand. Rooted in Faith.

In 2015, Seth Bolt and his father Larry hand-built a treehouse on 40 acres of farmland and forest as a honeymoon gift for Seth and his wife, Tori. What began as a private sanctuary quickly became something bigger.

"We realized how magical the experience is and how good it is for the soul," Seth says. They opened their doors to the public — and never looked back.

Today, Bolt Farm Treehouse is a Christ-centered luxury retreat destination with treehouses, domes, and mirror cabins nestled in the Tennessee mountains. Every detail is designed to help guests disconnect from the noise and reconnect — with each other, with nature, and with God.
