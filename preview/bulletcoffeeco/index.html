Founded by Birmingham native Brantley Visser, Bullet Coffee Co. was built on a simple idea: serve the highest quality coffee, face-to-face, with genuine connection. We're not a chain. We're your neighbors.

From our clean, well-organized drive-thru on US-280, we've become a neighborhood favorite across 13 Birmingham communities — and we're just getting started.
