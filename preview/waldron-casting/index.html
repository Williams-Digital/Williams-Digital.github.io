Who We Are

A Boutique Agency with Major Production Experience

Founded in 2016 by David Waldron, Waldron Casting is one of the foremost and most experienced background casting agencies in the tri-state area. Based in Manhattan, our team brings decades of combined experience from industry leaders including Grant Wilfley Casting, Central Casting, and Comer Casting.

In the fast-paced world of background casting, we've built a reputation for efficiency, organization, and clear communication — ensuring productions run smoothly while making sure every background actor feels valued and prepared.
