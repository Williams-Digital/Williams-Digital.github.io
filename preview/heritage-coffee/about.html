The Beginning

It Started With a Belief

Heritage Coffee didn’t start with a business plan. It started with a belief — that the Ensley Business District in Birmingham, Alabama deserves more than what it’s been given. It deserves a space where people come together, where conversations happen over good coffee, and where community isn’t just a word — it’s the whole point.

We chose Ensley because of its history, its resilience, and its people. This neighborhood has been through it all and keeps pushing forward. Heritage Coffee is part of that push.
