About Summit

Two Decades of Growth, Rooted in People

Founded in 2004, Summit Landscape Group has grown from a five-person crew into a multi-state enterprise managing over 1,200 commercial properties. Our success isn't measured only in acres maintained -- it's measured in careers launched, communities improved, and teams that choose to stay year after year.

In an industry where 51% of owners cite staffing as their top challenge, we've built something different. Our retention rate is nearly double the industry average because we invest in our people as aggressively as we invest in our equipment. Every crew member has a clear path forward, competitive compensation, and a team that has their back.

Careers at Summit

Join the team that's redefining what it means to work in the landscape industry.

51% of landscaping companies say staffing is their #1 challenge. At Summit, we've solved it -- not with gimmicks, but by building a workplace people don't want to leave. Competitive pay, real benefits, clear advancement, and a culture where every crew member matters.

Crew Leader

Full-Time 💰 $22 - $28/hr 📍 Multiple Locations 📅 Posted 3 days ago

Lead a crew of 4-6 technicians across commercial maintenance routes. Responsible for daily operations, quality control, client communication, and crew development. CDL preferred. Minimum 2 years supervisory experience.

Landscape Technician

Full-Time / Seasonal 💰 $17 - $22/hr 📍 Denver, CO 📅 Posted 1 week ago

Perform mowing, edging, pruning, mulching, and seasonal color rotations for commercial properties. Both full-time year-round and seasonal positions available. Training provided for entry-level candidates.

Irrigation Specialist

Full-Time 💰 $25 - $32/hr 📍 Phoenix, AZ 📅 Posted 5 days ago

Design, install, and maintain commercial irrigation systems including smart controllers, drip systems, and rotary heads. Troubleshoot system failures and manage water efficiency programs. Irrigation Technician certification required.

Equipment Operator

Full-Time 💰 $20 - $26/hr 📍 Dallas, TX 📅 Posted 2 days ago

Operate skid steers, mini excavators, loaders, and commercial mowing equipment for landscape construction and large-scale maintenance projects. CDL Class B required. Must pass equipment proficiency assessment.

Landscape Designer

Full-Time 💰 $55,000 - $75,000/yr 📍 Denver, CO 📅 Posted 1 week ago

Create landscape designs for commercial and high-end residential clients using AutoCAD, SketchUp, and Adobe Creative Suite. Conduct site analyses, present to clients, and collaborate with construction crews. BLA or equivalent preferred.

Seasonal Crew Member

Seasonal (Mar - Nov) 💰 $16 - $20/hr 📍 Multiple Locations 📅 Posted today

Join our seasonal team for the peak growing season. Perform mowing, trimming, blowing, and general grounds maintenance. No experience necessary -- we provide full training, equipment, and a path to year-round employment for top performers.

Certified Arborist

Full-Time 💰 $28 - $38/hr 📍 Denver, CO 📅 Posted 4 days ago

Manage tree care operations including health assessments, pruning, removal, and planting programs. ISA Certified Arborist credential required. Lead a specialized tree crew and consult on preservation plans for commercial clients.
