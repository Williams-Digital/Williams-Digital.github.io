About Apex

Revolutionizing the Landscape Industry

For 25 years, Apex Landscape Solutions has been transforming outdoor spaces. But we recognized a deeper truth: the landscaping industry's greatest challenge isn't design or execution -- it's people. With 51% of owners citing staffing as their #1 challenge and 80% struggling with hiring, we built something different.

Our platform combines world-class landscape services with the industry's first integrated workforce management system. We've reduced our turnover from the industry average of 35% to under 18%, saving millions in replacement costs and delivering consistently superior results.
