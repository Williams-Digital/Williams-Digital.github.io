1 Executive Summary

MGC Sports has built an incredible legacy over 40+ years representing elite NFL athletes. However, your current digital presence doesn't reflect the premium, professional service you provide. This proposal outlines a comprehensive rebrand designed to:

  • Establish digital authority with a modern, professional website that matches your brand reputation
  • Streamline client communication through a secure player portal with messaging and scheduling
  • Attract top talent by showcasing your unique value proposition (law firm backing, 40+ years experience)
  • Enhance NIL services positioning to capture the growing college athlete market

2 Scope of Work

The project includes two major deliverables:

A. Complete Website Rebrand

  • Modern, responsive design optimized for all devices
  • Premium sports-focused aesthetic with elegant gold and navy color scheme
  • Strategic content structure highlighting your unique differentiators
  • Dedicated NFL representation and NIL services sections
  • Professional contact forms with lead capture
  • SEO optimization for sports agency keywords
  • Fast loading speeds and Core Web Vitals optimization

B. Secure Player Portal

  • Private, secure login for each client athlete
  • Direct messaging system with agent and support team
  • Meeting scheduling with calendar integration
  • Document management (contracts, agreements, etc.)
  • Resource library with educational content
  • Profile and notification settings
