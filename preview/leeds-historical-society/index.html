Our Mission

Keeping Leeds' Story Alive for Generations

We promote the study, learning, and research of historical subjects while supporting the location, preservation, and restoration of historical sites, buildings, and structures of architectural interest throughout Leeds.

📚

Education & Research

Classes, lectures, seminars, and courses of instruction open to all community members.

🏘

Historic Preservation

Locating, preserving, and restoring buildings, residences, and areas of historical significance.

👥

Community Engagement

Engaging citizens and visitors through interactive activities that highlight local heritage.
