Why Choose Us

Why Southern Union?

An open admission, public two-year college providing quality and relevant teaching and learning that is affordable, accessible, equitable, and responsive.

Affordability

One of the most affordable colleges in Alabama. Access quality education without the burden of excessive student debt.

Active Campus Life

Join student clubs and organizations, SGA, fine arts programs, and enjoy residential life across our beautiful campuses.

Dual & Concurrent Enrollment

Get a head start on your college career while still in high school. Save money and transfer smoothly.

Scholarships

Multiple scholarship opportunities available to help make your education even more accessible and affordable.
