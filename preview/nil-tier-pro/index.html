Who We Are

More Than Agents. We're Your Team.

Founded in 2022, Apex Athlete Group was built on a simple belief: every athlete deserves a team that fights as hard off the field as they do on it. Based in Nashville, we're a boutique agency that combines data-driven strategy with genuine personal relationships.

From brand deals and social media management to compliance support and financial planning — we handle the business so our athletes can focus on what they do best.
