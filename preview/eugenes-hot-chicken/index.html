OUR STORY

From Nashville Roots to Birmingham's Big Red Truck

Founder Zebbie Carney grew up in Nashville eating at Prince's Hot Chicken. Every trip home reminded him that Birmingham needed real Nashville-style hot chicken.

He started at the library — writing a business plan, designing a logo. In his apartment, he perfected the spice blend. Through Create Birmingham's Co.Starters program, he turned a dream into a food truck in 2015.

In 2018, Eugene's opened the first brick-and-mortar in Birmingham's Uptown district. Today, three locations and two food trucks later, Eater.com named us the only Alabama restaurant on their "Best Fried Chicken in America" list.

"When you've got to have hot chicken done right, look for the big red truck."
