About GreenBlade

Trusted Landscaping Professionals Since 2019

Founded in 2019 by a team passionate about the outdoors, GreenBlade Landscaping has grown from a small neighborhood operation into one of the area's most trusted landscaping services. We combine old-fashioned hard work with modern techniques to deliver results that speak for themselves.

From routine lawn maintenance to full garden redesigns, every job receives the same level of care and attention to detail. We treat your property as if it were our own, because we believe every home deserves a landscape that brings joy.

We proudly serve homeowners and small businesses throughout the greater metropolitan area, including Maplewood, Oakdale, Riverdale, and surrounding communities.

Our Services

From routine maintenance to complete landscape transformations, we have you covered.

Lawn Mowing

Regular mowing services with precision cutting and clean edges. We keep your lawn at the ideal height for healthy, lush growth all season long.

Hedge Trimming

Expert shaping and trimming of hedges, shrubs, and ornamental plants. We create clean lines and maintain the natural beauty of your greenery.

Garden Design

Custom garden layouts tailored to your taste and property. From flower beds to vegetable gardens, we bring your vision to life with thoughtful planning.

Mulching

Premium mulch installation to retain moisture, suppress weeds, and give your garden beds a polished, professional look throughout the year.

Leaf Cleanup

Thorough fall and spring cleanups to clear leaves and debris. We keep your property looking neat and protect your lawn from seasonal damage.

Sod Installation

Fresh sod laid with expert precision for an instant green lawn. We prepare the soil properly to ensure strong root growth and long-lasting results.
