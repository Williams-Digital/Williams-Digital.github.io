FAQ

Questions?
We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know about working with Summit Sports Group.

What types of athletes do you work with?
We work with athletes at every level — from rising college stars navigating NIL to professional athletes looking to elevate their brand. Whether you're in basketball, football, soccer, track, or any other sport, we tailor our approach to your unique needs and goals.
How does the NIL process work?
We guide you through every step of the NIL landscape. From understanding your school's policies to connecting you with compliant brand partners, we ensure every deal is legal, profitable, and aligned with your personal brand. Our legal team reviews every contract.
What's included in your content packages?
Our content packages include professional photography, video production, social media management, graphic design, and copywriting. We create everything from game-day highlights to lifestyle content, ensuring a consistent and engaging presence across all your platforms.
How do I get started?
Getting started is simple. Fill out our contact form or reach out directly, and we'll schedule a free consultation call. We'll discuss your goals, evaluate your current brand, and create a custom strategy tailored to your career. No commitments required for the initial call.
What makes Summit different from other agencies?
We're built by athletes, for athletes. Our team has walked in your shoes, so we understand the unique challenges you face. We combine that firsthand experience with cutting-edge marketing strategies, giving you an agency that truly gets you — not just your stats.
