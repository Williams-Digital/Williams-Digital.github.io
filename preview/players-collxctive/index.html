Who We Are

More Than Management.
We Build Empires.

Players Collxctive is a full-service athlete management agency headquartered in Los Angeles, built exclusively for female athletes. In an industry where women's sports have been overlooked and undervalued, we refuse to play by the old rules.

From Olympic gymnasts to All-American softball stars, we represent the most dynamic women in sports. We negotiate harder, brand smarter, and fight louder — because our athletes deserve nothing less.
