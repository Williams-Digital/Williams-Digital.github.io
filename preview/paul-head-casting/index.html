About

The Person Behind the Casting

Paul Head
Casting Director • CSA

Paul Head

Vice President, Bodega Pictures

Raised in a small mountain town in Oregon, Paul developed an early love for storytelling, competing in historical documentary competitions through middle and high school and eventually competing nationally. He later relocated to Southern California to pursue film studies.

A graduate of Chapman University with a BFA in Film Production, Paul is a member of the Casting Society of America and received an Artios Award nomination for best unstructured casting on "sMothered."

He specializes in discovering compelling personalities and developing unscripted content, with a focus on identifying dynamic talent, shaping narratives, and bringing innovative concepts to television.
