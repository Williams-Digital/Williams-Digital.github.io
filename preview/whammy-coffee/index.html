Who We Are

Not your average
coffee shop.

We're Whammy Coffee — a Birmingham-based specialty coffee roaster and cafe nestled in the Denham Building. We don't take ourselves too seriously, but we take our coffee very seriously.

Every bag comes with a toy dinosaur. Every cup is made with care. Every visit should feel like hanging with ur homies.

"Rather than tasting notes on our bags, we write what the coffee reminds us of — 70s shag carpet, Sunday cartoons, things like that."

Free dino in every bag!
