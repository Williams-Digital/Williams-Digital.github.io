Overview

Favorable State Laws,
Formidable Protection

Not all states are created equal when it comes to asset protection. Several U.S. jurisdictions have enacted legislation that makes them exceptionally favorable for trusts, LLCs, and other protective structures. At JE Holdings, we harness these domestic advantages to build protection strategies that are both powerful and straightforward.

Our onshore planning expertise encompasses domestic asset protection trusts (DAPTs) in states like Nevada, Wyoming, Delaware, and South Dakota -- jurisdictions with no state income tax, strong privacy laws, perpetual trust provisions, and robust charging order protections. We also leverage series LLCs, land trusts, and specialized entity structures unique to these favorable states.

For clients who prefer the simplicity and familiarity of domestic structures -- or who want to build a strong onshore foundation before exploring international options -- our domestic planning provides proven, well-established frameworks backed by decades of legal precedent.
