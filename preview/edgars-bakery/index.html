Our Story

A Calling to
Create & Serve

"God was calling me to move into my own business — and I obeyed."

— Terry Smith, Founder

In 1998, Terry and Dottie Smith opened a small neighborhood bakery in Birmingham, Alabama with a simple promise: exceptional ingredients, genuine hospitality. Over 25 years later, that promise has grown into 11 locations across the state — and a loyal following that travels from New York to Miami just to taste a slice.

Edgar's has been featured in Taste of the South Magazine, Alabama Magazine, and Birmingham Magazine, but the biggest reward remains the smiles across the counter every morning.
