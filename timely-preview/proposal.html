Current State

What We Found

We conducted a thorough review of timelyent.com to understand where the current site stands and where the biggest opportunities are. Below are the key issues we identified — each of which is addressed in the redesigned tiers we've built for you.

1

Broken Pages

The "Helpful Links" page returns a 404 error, creating a dead end for visitors exploring the site.

2

Empty Events Calendar

The Events page displays "No upcoming events" with a completely empty calendar — an entertainment company without events raises questions.

3

Outdated Content

The most recent recognitions and highlights date back to 2009-2013 — over a decade old. The site doesn't reflect where Timely is today.

4

No Contact Form

The contact page only lists a phone number and email address with no way for visitors to submit an inquiry directly from the site.

5

Low-Resolution Hero Images

Homepage slider images are 960x400px — appearing blurry and pixelated on modern retina displays and larger screens.

6

No Calls-to-Action

There are no buttons or prompts guiding visitors toward the next step — no "Book Now," "Learn More," or "Get in Touch."

7

Outdated WordPress Theme

The Striking theme is visually dated and lacks the polish expected by prospective clients and partners in 2026.

8

Poor Mobile Experience

Navigation and layout break down on smaller screens. With over half of web traffic on mobile, this is costing visibility.

9

Heavy Page Weight

The Slider Revolution plugin and excessive inline JavaScript slow down load times, hurting both user experience and search rankings.

None of these issues are uncommon. Many businesses outgrow their original website without realizing it. The good news is every one of these problems has been solved in the previews we've built for you below.
