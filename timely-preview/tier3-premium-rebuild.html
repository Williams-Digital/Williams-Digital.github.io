The Founder

Rupert Francisco

Founder & Managing Director

With 27+ years in entertainment management and a law degree from UCLA School of Law, Rupert Francisco brings a rare fusion of legal acumen, business savvy, and deep industry relationships to every client engagement.

As the first African American attorney to establish a practice in Century City, Rupert shattered barriers while building a reputation as one of Los Angeles' most trusted entertainment advisors. His specialty in asset protection ensures that the empires his clients build are fortified for the long term.
