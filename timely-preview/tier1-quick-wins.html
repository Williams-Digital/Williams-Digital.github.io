A full-service artist and athlete management company headquartered in Los Angeles.

Founded by Rupert Francisco, Timely Entertainment, LLC provides comprehensive management services for artists and athletes at every stage of their careers. From emerging talent to established names, we focus on long-term career strategy, brand development, and building legacies that endure.

Operating from The Howard Hughes Center in the heart of Los Angeles, our team brings deep industry relationships and a results-driven approach to every client partnership.
