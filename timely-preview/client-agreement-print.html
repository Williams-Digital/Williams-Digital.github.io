01

Parties

This Client Service Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into by and between:

Service Provider

A digital services and web development firm specializing in website design, development, hosting, maintenance, and related digital solutions.

Client

Timely Entertainment, LLC, an artist and athlete management company.

02

Scope of Services

The Service Provider agrees to provide the Client with professional digital services, which may include but are not limited to:

  • Website Design & Development — Custom design and development of responsive, professional websites tailored to the Client's brand identity and business objectives.
  • Website Hosting & Deployment — Setup, configuration, and management of web hosting environments including domain configuration and SSL certification.
  • Website Maintenance & Updates — Ongoing maintenance, content updates, feature enhancements, bug fixes, and performance optimization.
  • Brand & Visual Identity — Development of digital brand elements including logos, color systems, typography, and visual assets for web and digital use.
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO) — On-page optimization, metadata configuration, and best practices to improve search engine visibility.
  • Digital Consulting — Strategic guidance on digital presence, technology selection, and online business solutions.

The specific services to be provided shall be determined in consultation between the Service Provider and the Client and may be further detailed in a separate Statement of Work or engagement letter.

Revision Policy

The initial project includes up to three (3) rounds of revisions during the design and development phase. A revision round consists of a consolidated set of feedback submitted at one time. Additional revision rounds beyond the included three will be billed at the Service Provider's hourly rate of $75/hour. Revisions must be requested within seven (7) days of receiving a design proof or deliverable; otherwise, the deliverable will be considered approved.

Change Orders & Scope Changes

Any work requested by the Client that falls outside the originally agreed-upon scope of services will require a written Change Order. The Service Provider will provide a quote for the additional work, and no work will commence until the Client approves the Change Order in writing. Change Orders may affect the project timeline and will be billed separately from the original setup fee.

03

Fees and Payment

In consideration of the services provided, the Client agrees to compensate the Service Provider as follows:

Setup Fee

A one-time setup fee of $800.00 USD is required for the initial website design, development, and deployment. This covers custom design, responsive development, hosting configuration, and launch of the completed website. The setup fee is payable in two installments: 50% deposit to begin, and 50% upon completion.

Monthly Service Plans

Following the initial setup, the Client shall select one of the following monthly service plans:

  • Basic — $50/month — Hosting, security updates, uptime monitoring, and up to 1 hour of content updates per month.
  • Standard — $100/month — Everything in Basic, plus up to 3 hours of content updates, design tweaks, performance optimization, and monthly analytics reporting.
  • Premium — $200/month — Everything in Standard, plus up to 6 hours of development time, new feature implementation, SEO optimization, and priority support.

Additional work beyond the included hours will be billed at $75/hour. The Client may upgrade or downgrade their plan with 30 days written notice.

Payment Methods

Payment shall be submitted via the secure payment link provided by the Service Provider. Payment details and instructions will be included on each invoice.

Payment Terms

A 50% deposit of the one-time setup fee ($400.00 USD) is required to begin the project. This deposit initiates the discovery, planning, and development process, including gathering all information necessary to take the Client's new website live. The remaining 50% balance ($400.00 USD) is due upon completion and delivery of the finished website.

The selected monthly service plan begins upon receipt of the initial deposit, enabling the Service Provider to set up hosting, domain configuration, and all supporting infrastructure from day one.

Monthly service fees are billed on the first of each month and are due upon receipt. A late fee of 1.5% per month may be assessed on any balance remaining unpaid beyond thirty (30) days from the invoice date. The Service Provider reserves the right to suspend services — including website hosting and maintenance — for any account with an outstanding balance exceeding sixty (60) days.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Should the project be cancelled by the Client after work has commenced, the 50% deposit is non-refundable as it covers discovery, planning, and development work already performed. If the project is cancelled by the Service Provider, the full deposit will be refunded to the Client.

Intellectual Property & Ownership

Upon receipt of full payment for the setup fee and any outstanding balances, the Client shall receive ownership of all custom-designed deliverables, including website files, graphics, and content created specifically for the Client. The Service Provider retains the right to use general techniques, tools, and methodologies developed or utilized during the engagement. Third-party assets (fonts, stock images, plugins) remain subject to their respective license agreements.

Portfolio & Credit Rights

The Service Provider retains the right to display and reference the completed website and related deliverables in their professional portfolio, marketing materials, case studies, and social media for the purpose of showcasing their work. This includes screenshots, descriptions of the project scope, and links to the live website. The Client agrees that a small, unobtrusive credit line (e.g., "Designed by [Service Provider]") may be placed in the website footer. If the Client wishes to opt out of the portfolio display or credit line, this must be agreed upon in writing prior to the commencement of work.

Domain & Hosting Ownership

Any domain names registered on behalf of the Client shall be owned by the Client. The Service Provider will register domains under the Client's name and contact information. Hosting accounts and server configurations managed by the Service Provider remain under the Service Provider's administrative control for the duration of the monthly service plan. Upon termination of the Agreement and settlement of all outstanding balances, the Service Provider will assist in transferring domain registration and hosting access to the Client or their designated new provider within a reasonable timeframe.

04

Confidentiality

Both parties acknowledge that in the course of this engagement, confidential and proprietary information may be disclosed. Each party agrees to:

  • Maintain the strict confidentiality of all information received from the other party.
  • Not disclose, reproduce, or distribute any confidential information to any third party without the prior written consent of the disclosing party.
  • Use confidential information solely for the purposes of fulfilling obligations under this Agreement.
  • Take reasonable precautions to protect the confidentiality of the information, no less protective than those used for their own confidential information.

This confidentiality obligation shall survive the termination of this Agreement and continue for a period of five (5) years following termination or expiration.

05

Client Responsibilities

To facilitate the effective delivery of services, the Client agrees to:

  • Provide timely, accurate, and complete content, assets, and information as reasonably requested by the Service Provider.
  • Respond to communications, design proofs, and requests for approval within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Designate a primary point of contact for all matters relating to this engagement.
  • Review and provide feedback on deliverables within the timeframes specified, understanding that delayed feedback may extend project timelines.
  • Ensure that all content, images, and materials provided to the Service Provider for use in deliverables are owned by the Client or properly licensed.
  • Maintain current payment information for any recurring services such as hosting or maintenance.

Delays or failure to meet these responsibilities may impact the timeline and effectiveness of the services provided, and the Service Provider shall not be held liable for any resulting delays or diminished outcomes.

Project Pause & Client Inactivity

If the Client becomes unresponsive or fails to provide requested materials, feedback, or approvals for a period of fourteen (14) consecutive days, the project will be placed on hold. The Service Provider will notify the Client in writing that the project has been paused. A reactivation fee of $150.00 USD may apply to resume work after a project pause, as the Service Provider may need to re-familiarize with the project and reallocate resources. Projects paused for more than sixty (60) days may be treated as cancelled under the Cancellation & Refund Policy outlined in Section 3.

Launch Sign-Off

Prior to the public launch of the website, the Service Provider will present the completed website to the Client for final review and approval. The Client shall have five (5) business days to review the website and provide written approval to proceed with launch, or submit final revision requests within the included revision rounds. If the Client does not respond within this review period, the website will be deemed approved and the Service Provider may proceed with launch. Once launch approval is given (or deemed given), the Client acknowledges the website as delivered and the remaining 50% balance becomes due.

06

Term and Termination

This Agreement shall become effective upon acceptance by the Client and shall remain in effect until the completion of the agreed-upon services, unless earlier terminated as follows:

Termination by Either Party

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing fourteen (14) days written notice to the other party. Written notice shall be delivered via email to the designated contact addresses. Upon termination, the Service Provider will provide the Client with all completed deliverables and assist with a reasonable transition period.

Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party materially breaches any provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within five (5) days of receiving written notice of the breach.

Effect of Termination

Upon termination, the Client shall pay the Service Provider for all services rendered and expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination. The Service Provider will deliver all completed work product and provide reasonable assistance for transition to another provider. All confidentiality obligations shall survive termination as specified in Section 4.

Dispute Resolution

In the event of any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement, the parties agree to first attempt to resolve the matter through good faith negotiation. If the dispute cannot be resolved through negotiation within thirty (30) days, the parties agree to submit the dispute to binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

The arbitration shall be conducted in the United States, and the decision of the arbitrator shall be final and binding upon both parties. Each party shall bear its own costs and expenses related to the arbitration, and the costs of the arbitrator shall be shared equally.
