Timely Entertainment, LLC is an artist and athlete management company headquartered in Los Angeles, California. Founded by Rupert Francisco, the company represents a diverse roster of talented performers across film, television, music, and athletics.

Distinguished as the only management company led by a Manager who is also an Asset Protection specialist, Timely Entertainment brings a unique combination of creative management and financial expertise to every client relationship.

Rupert Francisco distinguishes himself as the only Manager in the entertainment business who is also an Asset Protection specialist. Over 27 years, he has been involved in structuring and negotiating more than one thousand recording contracts and television/motion picture transactions.

An honors graduate of the University of California, Riverside (1977), Francisco interned in California Lt. Governor Mervyn Dymally's office before earning his J.D. from UCLA School of Law. He practiced law in California from 1983 to 1989, becoming the first and youngest African American attorney to establish a law practice in Century City in 1983.

He led The Law Offices of Rupert Francisco from 1 to 3 locations and went on to found J E Holdings, LLC — the only African-American owned Asset Protection Firm in the United States — as well as Tenland, Inc., the first and only company of its kind in the world.
