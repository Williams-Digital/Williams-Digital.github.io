Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book? ▼

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons (holidays, spring break, and summer). For last-minute needs, reach out to us — we'll do our best to accommodate your royal request!

Are your nannies background checked? ▼

Absolutely. Every Nannyland nanny undergoes a comprehensive vetting process including FBI-level background checks, reference verification, CPR/First Aid certification, and completion of our exclusive Royal Academy training program.

What ages do your nannies care for? ▼

Our nannies care for children of all ages — from newborns to teenagers. We match families with nannies who specialize in your children's specific age group and needs.

Can the nanny join us inside the theme parks? ▼

Yes! Our Theme Park Nanny service is designed for exactly that. Your nanny will accompany your family inside the parks. You'll provide a park ticket for the nanny, and they'll help manage the kids, hold your place in lines, and keep little ones entertained.

What is your cancellation policy? ▼

We understand plans change! Cancellations made 72+ hours before your booking are fully refundable. Cancellations within 24-72 hours receive a 50% refund. Please see our full policy for details.
