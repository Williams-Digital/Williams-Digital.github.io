Our Story

Iron & Blade was born from a simple belief: every man deserves to look and feel his best. Founded in a small storefront in 2016, we've grown into the city's premier destination for the modern gentleman.

We blend old-school barbering traditions — hot towel shaves, straight razor fades, and genuine craftsmanship — with a modern atmosphere that feels like home. Walk in as a customer, leave as family.
