The State Bird of Alabama

Fly High,
Hammer Down

The yellowhammer — Alabama's state bird — is a Northern Flicker known for the flash of brilliant gold beneath its wings. It's a bird that doesn't just fly. It hammers.

We named our company after that spirit. Have spectacular dreams and work tirelessly to bring them to life. From a single coffee trailer in Semmes to seven thriving locations across Mobile, that's exactly what we've done.

Our Story

From a Trailer to Seven Locations

In 2016, Jeff and Monique Roberts had a dream: bring amazing coffee and unmatched service to Mobile, Alabama. It started with a black trailer, a bright yellow coffee-bean logo, and a whole lot of heart.

What began as a scrappy startup has grown into seven thriving locations across Mobile and Semmes. Through it all, one thing hasn't changed — the belief that the culture of the company should be just as important as the coffee.

Our motto says it all: Fly High, Hammer Down. Have spectacular dreams and work tirelessly to bring them to life.
