About This Location

Where City Meets Delta

Our Moffett Road location sits along the corridor connecting Mobile to Semmes, near one of the most biodiverse ecosystems in North America — the Mobile-Tensaw River Delta. It's a part of town where the energy of the city fades into the quiet beauty of nature.

Inside, you'll find the warm, inviting space that defines every Yellowhammer location. Great coffee, friendly faces, and a community-first atmosphere that keeps our neighbors coming back day after day.

Founded in 2016 by Jeff and Monique Roberts, Yellowhammer Coffee started from a trailer and a dream. Today, with seven locations and a motto that says it all — Fly High, Hammer Down — we're proud to serve the community we love.
