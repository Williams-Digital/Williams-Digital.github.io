About This Location

Where It All Started

The Bay City Trailer holds a special place in the Yellowhammer story. This is where it all began — a coffee trailer, a dream, and a commitment to serving the Semmes community with amazing coffee and unmatched hospitality.

Nestled on Blackwell Nursery Road in rural Semmes, this is the original Yellowhammer location. No fancy storefront, just genuinely great coffee served from a trailer with heart — and the kind of friendly service that makes you feel like family.

Founded in 2016 by Jeff and Monique Roberts, Yellowhammer Coffee started right here with a trailer and a dream. Today, with seven locations and a motto that says it all — Fly High, Hammer Down — we're proud to still serve the community where it all began.
