About This Location

Where It All Started

The Bay City Trailer holds a special place in the Yellowhammer story. This is where it all began — a coffee trailer, a dream, and a commitment to serving the Semmes community with amazing coffee and unmatched hospitality.

Named after the spirit of the local community and inspired by the heritage of Semmes — home to the historic 1902 Schoolhouse at Semmes Heritage Park — this location keeps it real. No frills, just genuinely great coffee and the kind of friendly service that makes you feel like family.

Founded in 2016 by Jeff and Monique Roberts, Yellowhammer Coffee started from a trailer and a dream. Today, with seven locations and a motto that says it all — Fly High, Hammer Down — we're proud to serve the community we love.
