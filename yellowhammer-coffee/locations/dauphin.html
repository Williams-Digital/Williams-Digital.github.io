About This Location

Historic Dauphin Street

Nestled on one of Mobile's most beloved streets, our Dauphin location sits in the heart of midtown's cultural corridor. Dauphin Street's French Colonial architecture and oak-lined sidewalks create the perfect backdrop for your daily coffee ritual.

Step inside and you'll find the same warm, welcoming atmosphere that defines every Yellowhammer location — exceptional coffee, genuine hospitality, and a space that feels like home. Whether it's your morning espresso or an afternoon pick-me-up, we're here to make it a wow moment.

Founded in 2016 by Jeff and Monique Roberts, Yellowhammer Coffee started from a trailer and a dream. Today, with seven locations and a motto that says it all — Fly High, Hammer Down — we're proud to serve the community we love.
