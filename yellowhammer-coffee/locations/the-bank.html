About This Location

West Mobile's Favorite

Located on Cottage Hill Road in the heart of west Mobile, The Bank brings Yellowhammer Coffee to one of the city's busiest corridors. Just a short drive from the USS Alabama Battleship Memorial Park, this location has become a neighborhood staple.

The Bank offers a comfortable, inviting atmosphere where you can enjoy expertly crafted coffee and genuine Southern hospitality. Whether you're grabbing a quick espresso on the go or settling in for a study session, our team makes every visit memorable.

Founded in 2016 by Jeff and Monique Roberts, Yellowhammer Coffee started from a trailer and a dream. Today, with seven locations and a motto that says it all — Fly High, Hammer Down — we're proud to serve the community we love.
