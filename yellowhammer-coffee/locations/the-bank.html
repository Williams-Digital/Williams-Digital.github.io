What Makes Us Special

Dine in the Vault

The Bank is a stunning re-adaptive reuse of an old bank building on Cottage Hill Road. Step inside and you'll find a one-of-a-kind dining area housed within the former vault — a truly unique space where history meets great coffee.

With an inviting outdoor patio and extended evening hours, The Bank is the first Yellowhammer location where you can enjoy your favorite drinks and bites well into the evening. Whether you're meeting friends after work or winding down your day, we're here until 8 PM.
