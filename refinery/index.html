The Experience

A Speakeasy for the Modern South

Designed with deep colors and sharp Sylacauga white marble, The Refinery brings a medieval-modern atmosphere to Birmingham — inspired by New York's speakeasy era, rooted in Southern flavor.

Full lunch and dinner service, signature cocktails, and a curated selection of local craft beers and wines. This is where date night meets Birmingham soul.
