Our Story

FROM GARAGE TO TAPROOM

What started as two friends brewing in a garage has grown into one of Portland's most beloved craft breweries. We believe great beer comes from great ingredients, patience, and an obsessive attention to detail.

2016
First homebrew batch in a Southeast Portland garage
2018
Won Portland Beer Week People's Choice Award
2019
Opened our 5,000 sqft taproom & production brewery
2021
Expanded distribution to 200+ locations statewide
2024
GABF Silver Medal — Forge Lager
