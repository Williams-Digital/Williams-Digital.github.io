Our Story

Prestige Homes was founded in 1997 by Robert Mitchell with a simple mission: build homes that Alabama families can be proud of. What started as a small operation in Birmingham has grown into one of the state's most trusted home builders.

Over the past 25+ years, we've built more than 1,500 homes across 9 communities spanning from Huntsville to Prattville. Every home carries our commitment to quality construction, energy efficiency, and timeless design.

Today, Prestige Homes remains family-owned and locally operated. We're proud of our A+ BBB rating and the trust that thousands of Alabama families have placed in us.
