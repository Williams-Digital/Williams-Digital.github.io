See the Transformation

How key campus locations will change on gameday

Parking Lot Experience

Before

Fans drive in circles looking for spots. No indication which lots are full. Confusion about which direction leads to the stadium. Silent, disconnected from gameday energy.

After

Digital signs show real-time capacity: "Lot A: 80% Full → Lot C: Available." Directional arrows point toward stadium. Speakers play fight songs. The experience starts here.

Campus Walkways

Before

Quiet sidewalks with no indication a game is happening. First-time visitors ask for directions. No visual connection to Auburn spirit until reaching the stadium gates.

After

LED totems display "War Eagle!" and walking directions. Coordinated audio plays across zones. Illuminated pathway markers for evening games. Photo stations capture memories.

Tailgate Areas

Before

Individual tailgaters bring their own speakers. Cacophony of different music. No shared visual experience. Missed announcements about weather or schedule changes.

After

Zoned audio delivers consistent gameday soundtrack. Mobile LED screens show pregame shows and live updates. Emergency alerts reach everyone instantly. One unified celebration.

Emergency Situations

Before

Lightning delay announced only inside stadium. Outdoor fans unaware. Word spreads slowly by text message. No clear guidance on shelter locations.

After

All speakers and displays switch to alert mode in under 10 seconds. Visual directions to nearest shelter. Automated weather monitoring triggers proactive warnings.
