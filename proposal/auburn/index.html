Refresh Triggers (Beyond Time-Based)

  • Performance Degradation: Device no longer meets minimum performance standards
  • Security End-of-Life: OS or hardware no longer receiving security updates
  • Repair Cost Threshold: Repair costs exceed 40% of replacement value
  • Compatibility Issues: Unable to run required software/applications
  • Warranty Expiration: Out-of-warranty with high failure risk
  • Mission Change: Role requirements exceed device capabilities

Cascade Program

Devices with remaining useful life can be cascaded to lower-priority use cases, extending overall ROI while maintaining frontline device quality:

Year 1-2: Monitor & Maintain └── Regular updates, preventive maintenance, warranty coverage Year 3: Evaluate & Plan └── Performance assessment, budget allocation, procurement planning Year 4: Replace High-Priority └── Executive, faculty, high-use labs, student-facing devices Year 5: Complete Cycle └── Administrative, low-use devices, cascade eligible units Cascade Flow: Executive Laptops → Administrative Staff Faculty Laptops → Lab Computers (light use) Lab Computers → Training/Testing Environments
