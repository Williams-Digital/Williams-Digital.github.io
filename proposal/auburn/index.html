Campus Production Enhancement Initiative

Comprehensive AV/IT infrastructure modernization for gameday operations, classroom instruction, and campus-wide media production.

War Eagle! View Proposal

Executive Summary

This proposal outlines a comprehensive production enhancement initiative designed to transform Auburn University's campus into a state-of-the-art environment. The proposed solutions will improve safety, streamline content creation, enhance the learning experience, and position Auburn as a leader in educational technology innovation.

Scope Areas

Gameday Environment

Transform Jordan-Hare Stadium into a broadcast-ready environment with fiber backbone, wireless cameras, and LED integration.

People Tracking & Analytics

Intelligent people-counting with thermal sensors, stereo vision, and LiDAR for crowd management and facility optimization.

Q-SYS Ecosystem

Campus-wide networked AV infrastructure unifying audio, video, and control under a single scalable platform.

Stream Deck Integration

One-touch classroom control for instructors with intuitive interfaces for recording, lighting, and AV systems.

AI-Powered Cameras

Intelligent auto-tracking cameras with gesture recognition, voice-activated switching, and whiteboard detection.

Cloud Recording

Automated lecture capture with AI captioning, multi-platform distribution, and LMS integration.

Device Tracking & Management

Comprehensive asset management for 8,000+ computers, laptops, and mobile devices with Jamf, Intune, and ServiceNow.

Refresh Triggers Beyond Time

  • Performance Degradation: Below minimum standards
  • Security End-of-Life: No longer receiving updates
  • Repair Cost Threshold: Exceeds 40% of replacement
  • Compatibility Issues: Can't run required software
  • Warranty Expiration: High failure risk
