Risk Mitigation

Proactive strategies to address potential challenges and ensure project success

Timeline Delays

Medium Risk

Supply chain disruptions or vendor availability could impact equipment delivery timelines.

Mitigation Strategy
Early procurement of long-lead items, multiple vendor relationships, and 15% schedule buffer built into each phase.

Staff Adoption

Medium Risk

Faculty and staff resistance to new technology or workflows could slow adoption.

Mitigation Strategy
Phased training program, faculty champions program, 24/7 support hotline, and intuitive Stream Deck interfaces.

Integration Complexity

Low Risk

Multiple systems (Q-SYS, Jamf, Canvas, Panopto) require seamless integration.

Mitigation Strategy
Pre-validated integration playbooks, pilot testing in isolated environments, and dedicated integration sprint in Phase 1.

Budget Overruns

Low Risk

Unforeseen infrastructure requirements or scope changes could increase costs.

Mitigation Strategy
10% contingency built into estimates, fixed-price contracts where possible, and monthly budget reviews with stakeholders.

Network Capacity

Medium Risk

Existing network infrastructure may require upgrades to support new AV traffic.

Mitigation Strategy
Network assessment in Phase 1, VLAN segmentation for AV traffic, and partnership with campus IT for infrastructure upgrades.

Security & Privacy

Low Risk

People tracking and recording systems require careful privacy considerations.

Mitigation Strategy
FERPA-compliant design, opt-out mechanisms, data anonymization, and clear signage in monitored areas.
