Our Story

Crafted with Passion

Brew & Bean was born from a simple belief: great coffee brings people together. Since opening our doors in 2018, we've been dedicated to sourcing the finest single-origin beans and roasting them right here in Auburn.

Every cup we serve is crafted with intention, from our signature house blend to our rotating seasonal offerings. We partner with ethical farms across Latin America, Africa, and Southeast Asia.

Whether you're grabbing your morning espresso or settling in for an afternoon of work, Brew & Bean is your space.
