Our Story

Where Every Cup
Tells a Story

Brew & Bean started as a passion project between two friends who believed Auburn deserved better coffee. What began in a small garage with a second-hand roaster has grown into Auburn's go-to specialty coffee destination.

We source single-origin beans from small farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and beyond. Every batch is roasted in-house to bring out the unique character of each origin.

Our commitment goes beyond the cup. We're proudly zero-waste, use 100% compostable packaging, and donate 2% of profits to local community programs.

- The Brew & Bean Team
