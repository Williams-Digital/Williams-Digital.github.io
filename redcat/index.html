Our Philosophy

Specialty Coffee, No Pretension

We believe great coffee shouldn't come with a side of attitude. Red Cat is about specialty-grade coffees treated with care but made accessible — whether you're a pour-over purist or just want a damn good latte.

We roast our beans in-house, bake our goods from scratch (in partnership with Birmingham Breadworks), and keep the vibe welcoming for everyone who walks through our doors.
