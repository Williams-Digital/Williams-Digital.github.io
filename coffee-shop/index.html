Our Journey

From Bean to Cup

At Ember & Bloom, coffee isn't just a drink — it's a craft we've spent over a decade perfecting. Founded in 2013 in a tiny roastery on Elm Street, we set out with a simple mission: source the world's finest beans and roast them to bring out their unique character.

We partner directly with farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and Sumatra, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices. Every batch is small-lot roasted in-house, bringing out nuanced flavors that tell the story of their origin.

Today, our café is a neighborhood gathering place — a warm corner where conversations flow as freely as our espresso. Whether you're a pour-over purist or a latte lover, there's a cup here with your name on it.
