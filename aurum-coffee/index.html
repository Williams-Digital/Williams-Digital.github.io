Our Story

Born From
Obsession

AURUM began with a single belief: coffee is not just a drink — it's a ritual, a moment of luxury in the everyday. Founded by master roasters and flavor architects, we source the world's rarest beans from volcanic highlands, mist-covered plantations, and ancient growing regions.

Every cup at AURUM is a testament to our relentless pursuit of perfection. From the golden ratio of our pour-overs to the velvety crema on our espressos, we craft experiences that transcend the ordinary.

About Aurum

The People Behind
the Pour

AURUM was founded by Elena Marchetti and James Oduya — two people united by an obsessive love for coffee and an unwillingness to compromise on quality.

Elena, a third-generation Italian roaster, brings generations of Mediterranean coffee wisdom. James, a former sommelier turned coffee innovator, brings a palate trained on the world's finest flavors.

Together, they've built AURUM into a destination for those who believe that their morning cup should be nothing short of extraordinary.

Elena Marchetti Co-Founder & Head Roaster
