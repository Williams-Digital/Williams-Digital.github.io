The Story of
Jaylen Thompson

Born and raised in Atlanta, Georgia, Jaylen Thompson discovered his love for basketball at the age of five. By high school, he was the most sought-after point guard in the nation — a McDonald's All-American with elite court vision, a lightning-quick first step, and a natural ability to make everyone around him better.

At Duke University, Jaylen has elevated his game to an entirely new level. Leading the Blue Devils with 18.4 points and 8.2 assists per game, he's not just a player — he's a program-defining talent. Off the court, Jaylen has built a brand that resonates with millions, partnering with Beats by Dre, Champs Sports, and launching a signature shoe campaign that sold out in 48 hours.

"I don't just play to win games. I play to build something that lasts."
