Our Story

More Than Coffee.
A Community.

Red Cat Coffee House started with a simple belief: specialty-grade coffee should be accessible to everyone. We source, roast, and brew every bean with intention — because we think great coffee tastes even better in good company.

From our Pepper Place flagship to Railroad Park and UAB Highlands, each location is designed to be a welcoming space for Birmingham to gather, connect, and slow down.

Mike & Aileen McElwain
