About Warner Museums

Beyond the Narrative

Warner Museums partners with clients to tell their stories by creating engaging, inclusive and memorable experiences.

Through planning, design, build and installation, we transform interpretive plans into 3-dimensional interactive environments. We help museums and cultural institutions of 1,500 to 150,000 square feet create authentic stories and dimensional, multisensory experiences.

Civil Rights & African American History

Honoring the Past,
Inspiring the Future

The African American story is quintessentially an American one of determination, faith, perseverance, pride, and resilience.

A commitment to belonging and equality has profoundly shaped this history and culture. Our exhibits are designed to create emotional resonance, documenting significant civil rights narratives and evoking understanding at deeper levels.
