Our Curriculum

Abeka Christian Curriculum

C3 uses the nationally recognized Abeka curriculum, providing a structured, Bible-based approach to education. Our small class sizes (averaging just 5 students) allow teachers to provide one-on-one instruction tailored to each child's needs.

With state-registered teachers and a 5:1 student-teacher ratio, every student receives the individual attention needed to thrive academically and spiritually.

Core Subjects

Building Strong Foundations

Our comprehensive curriculum prepares students for academic success at every level.

English & Literature

English I-IV, grammar, writing, vocabulary, and literature studies developing strong communication skills through a biblical worldview.

Mathematics

Pre-Algebra, Algebra I & II, Geometry, and Financial Literacy — building problem-solving skills from elementary through high school.

Bible & Religion

Daily Bible instruction woven throughout every grade level — the cornerstone of our Christ-centered educational approach.

Inclusive Education

The D.R.E.A.M. Program

Determination · Reliable Education · Accountability · Ministry

Our D.R.E.A.M. program serves students with mild-to-moderate diagnoses, removing the bias that private schools often have against students with Individualized Education Plans (IEPs).

We believe every young person — regardless of ability — deserves equal opportunities with expert-led support in a caring, faith-based environment.

  • IEP-supported learning plans
  • ADD/ADHD and learning difference support
  • Disability Grant eligible (up to $17,000/year)
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
