Our Mission

Developing Full Academic Potential Within a Christian Environment

C3 Concord Christian School was founded in 2008 with a vision to introduce every child to Jesus Christ and nurture their growth — academically, spiritually, and personally.

"C3" stands for Committing, Changing, and Creating — a path of knowledge that shapes students into confident, compassionate leaders.

  • Christ-centered curriculum and character development
  • Small class sizes averaging just 5 students
  • Individualized attention for every learner

What We Offer

Programs Built Around Your Child

From early childhood to high school, our programs combine rigorous academics with faith-based character development.

Core Academics

Abeka curriculum covering English, Math, Science, Social Studies, and Literature from PreK through 12th grade with personalized instruction.

D.R.E.A.M. Program

Specialized support for students with mild-to-moderate learning differences. Expert-led, IEP-supported education where every child belongs.

C3 Sports Academy

Football, basketball, baseball, track & field, and more — with integrated college prep, SAT/ACT training, and scholarship guidance.

Arts & Enrichment

Art, drama, chorus, and foreign language (Spanish) round out our curriculum for a well-balanced educational experience.

College Preparation

SAT/ACT prep, college readiness workshops, NCAA guidance, campus tours, and personalized mentoring for high school students.

Before & After Care

Extended care from early morning drop-off through 6:00 PM, providing a safe, structured environment for working families.
