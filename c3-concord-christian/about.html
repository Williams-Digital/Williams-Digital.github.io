About C3 Concord Christian School

Founded in faith, built on community, and committed to developing every child's God-given potential.

Our Mission

Introducing Every Child to Jesus Christ

Our mission is to introduce every child to Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior of their lives and to nurture their Christian growth — developing full academic potential within a Christian environment.

We are centered on God, with a desire to develop well-rounded students where academic excellence is balanced by emotional intelligence, character, and faith.

"In Christ we have the victory."
— School Motto
The Meaning of C3

Committing. Changing. Creating.

Our name reflects the transformative journey every student takes at C3 — a path of knowledge that shapes confident, compassionate leaders.

Committing

Committing to Christ, to learning, and to being part of a community that values each individual's worth and purpose.

Changing

Growing and transforming through knowledge, discipline, and the development of both mind and character.

Creating

Creating a path of knowledge, building futures, and equipping students to make a positive impact on the world.

Our History

A Story Built on Faith

C3 Concord Christian School was founded in 2008 under the direction of Concord Christian Center by Dr. Cecil Brown and co-founder Faye Brown.

What began as a vision to provide quality Christian education to families in the Concord community has grown into a thriving PreK-12 school serving students from across the region.

The school was formed and integrated to merge learning through Committing, Changing, and Creating — giving rise to the "C3" name that represents our educational philosophy.

Today, C3 continues to uphold its founding principles while expanding programs like the C3 Sports Academy and the D.R.E.A.M. special needs initiative.

Our Church Family

A Ministry of Concord Christian Center

C3 Concord Christian School operates as a ministry of Concord Christian Center, originally incorporated as C&F Ministries Inc. in 2000 by Pastor Cecil and Faye Brown.

The church's vision is to preach and teach God's Word, to equip people, and to use God-given tools for knowledge, wisdom, and understanding of the Kingdom of God.

As part of this broader ministry ecosystem, the school benefits from the church's community programs including:

  • Concord Christian Daycare (ages 6 weeks+)
  • "Seeds of Blessings" Food Bank
  • Community outreach and senior ministry programs
