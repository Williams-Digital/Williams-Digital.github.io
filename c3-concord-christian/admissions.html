Getting Started

How to Apply

Our admissions process is straightforward. Follow these steps to begin your child's journey at C3.

1

Submit Application

Complete the Application for Admission with the $200 application fee. Contact our admissions office to receive forms.

2

Gather Documents

Prepare required documents: birth certificate, immunization records, transcripts, and report cards.

3

Interview

Meet with the principal for a personal interview. Scheduled once your application is reviewed.

4

Enrollment

Receive your acceptance and complete enrollment. Welcome to the C3 family!

Questions

Admissions FAQ

What grades do you serve? ▼

C3 serves students from PreK-4 through 12th grade. We also offer a Christian daycare program for children as young as 6 weeks through our partnership with Concord Christian Daycare.

Is there a deadline to apply? ▼

We accept applications on a rolling basis year-round. Currently enrolled students have priority re-enrollment through March 1, after which registration is first-come, first-served.

Do you accept students with learning differences? ▼

Yes! Our D.R.E.A.M. program (Determination, Reliable Education with Accountability through Ministry) serves students with mild-to-moderate diagnoses and IEPs. Students may also qualify for the Disability Grant of up to $17,000 annually.

What financial aid is available? ▼

We accept the NC Opportunity Scholarship (up to $9,000/year), the Disability Grant (up to $17,000/year), and the Education Savings Account (ESA+). Sibling discounts are also available. Contact our admissions office for details.

What are the school hours? ▼

Instructional hours are 8:30 AM to 2:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Before-school care is available, and after-school care runs from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM.

Can I schedule a campus tour? ▼

Absolutely! We encourage all prospective families to visit our campus. Contact us at (704) 788-4726 or visit our contact page to schedule a tour.

Ready to Begin?

Contact our admissions team today. We're happy to answer your questions, schedule a tour, or help you start the application process.
