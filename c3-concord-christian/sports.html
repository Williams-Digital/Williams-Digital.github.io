C3 Sports Academy

A holistic approach to student-athlete development — building champions on the field, in the classroom, and in life.

Our Philosophy

More Than Just Athletics

The C3 Sports Academy takes a holistic approach to student-athlete development. We believe that athletics, academics, and character development go hand in hand.

Our program equips young athletes with elite sports instruction, academic readiness, and the personal development skills needed to succeed at the next level — whether that's college athletics or professional life.

  • Academic readiness alongside athletic training
  • Qualified trainers and coaching staff
  • Personal character and life skills development
  • College scholarship guidance and NCAA prep
Our Sports

Programs & Teams

From varsity competition to interscholastic leagues, we offer a range of athletic programs for student-athletes of all levels.

Football

Varsity program with 7-on-7 league competition. Building teamwork, discipline, and athletic excellence on the gridiron.

Basketball

Competitive program including AAU team participation. Developing court skills, strategy, and sportsmanship.

Track & Field

Individual and team competition developing speed, endurance, and personal achievement across multiple events.

Baseball

Competitive baseball program building fundamental skills, teamwork, and a love for America's pastime.

Dance

Interscholastic dance program fostering artistic expression, coordination, and confidence through performance.

Flag Football

Interscholastic flag football program — a great introduction to the sport with an emphasis on fun and fundamentals.

College Bound

College Preparation for Student-Athletes

We equip our athletes with the tools, knowledge, and support to compete at the collegiate level.

SAT/ACT Prep

Structured test preparation classes to help student-athletes achieve competitive scores for college admissions.

NCAA Guidance

Education on NCAA eligibility requirements, academic standards, and the recruiting process for aspiring college athletes.

Scholarship Support

Athletic scholarship guidance and financial aid navigation to help families plan for collegiate athletics.

College Tours

Organized campus visits to help student-athletes explore their options and find the right fit for their goals.

Mentoring & Tutoring

Ongoing academic mentoring and tutoring to ensure athletes maintain eligibility and academic excellence.

Career Readiness

Life skills and career preparation for athletes who plan to pursue professional pathways beyond college sports.

Join the C3 Sports Academy

Whether your child is an aspiring athlete or looking to try something new, our Sports Academy offers opportunities for growth, competition, and fun.
