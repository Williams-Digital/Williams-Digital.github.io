Our Story

Maison Claire was born from a belief that style should be effortless, sustainable, and deeply personal. Founded by Claire Beaumont in 2019, we curate a collection of timeless pieces from emerging and established designers who share our values.

"I wanted to create a space where every piece tells a story and every woman feels seen."

We partner with artisans and ethical brands from around the world, prioritizing natural fabrics, fair production, and designs that transcend seasons. Each piece in our boutique is hand-selected for its quality, versatility, and beauty.
