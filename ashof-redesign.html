Our Mission

Honoring Alabama's Greatest Athletes

The Alabama Sports Hall of Fame serves as a testament to the extraordinary athletic achievements born from the state of Alabama. From legendary coaches to Olympic champions, our museum preserves the stories that shaped sports history.

Located in the heart of Birmingham, our 33,000-square-foot museum houses interactive exhibits, memorabilia, and multimedia presentations that bring Alabama's sports legacy to life.
