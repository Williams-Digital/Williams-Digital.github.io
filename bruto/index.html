Our Story

Southern Spirit, Italian Soul

From the same team behind Mom's Basement, Bruto brings something new to Avondale — an Italian-inspired café built on warm hospitality, exceptional coffee, and house-made everything.

The name? "Bruto" is Italian for "critter" — because in the South, we call animals critters. It's that blend of worlds that defines everything we do, from our focaccia to our espresso.

Step inside to calming green walls, eclectic curated art, a 23-foot oriental rug, and a leather-topped community table built for gathering.
